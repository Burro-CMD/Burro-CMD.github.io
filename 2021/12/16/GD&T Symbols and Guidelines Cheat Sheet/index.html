GD&T Symbols and Guidelines Cheat Sheet

Posted on 2021-12-16 In Tech Views: Word count in article: 3.2k Reading time ≈ 3 mins.
GD&T（Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing）是一种用于精确描述零件尺寸、形状、方向和位置的工程符号语言，旨在确保零件装配精度、提高质量并降低制造成本。

STANDARD: ASME Y14.5-2018

GEOMETRIC CHARACTERISTIC SYMBOLS（14 Symbols）

1) FORM（No Datum）

  • Straightness —— 直线度
    Symbol: —
    Meaning: Element lies between two parallel lines.
    Zone: 2 parallel lines.
  • Flatness —— 平面度
    Symbol: ▭
    Meaning: Surface between two parallel planes.
    Zone: 2 parallel planes.
  • Circularity (Roundness) —— 圆度
    Symbol: ◯
    Meaning: Cross-section between two concentric circles.
    Zone: 2 concentric circles.
  • Cylindricity —— 圆柱度
    Symbol: ⌭
    Meaning: Entire cylinder between two concentric cylinders.
    Zone: 2 concentric cylinders.
    2) PROFILE（Datum optional）
  • Profile of a Line —— 线轮廓度
    Symbol: ⌒
    Meaning: Each line element within tolerance band.
    Zone: Band along true profile.
  • Profile of a Surface —— 面轮廓度
    Symbol: ⌓
    Meaning: Entire surface within tolerance band.
    Zone: 3D band around true profile.
    3) ORIENTATION（Require Datum）
  • Parallelism —— 平行度
    Symbol: ||
    Meaning: Parallel to datum plane/axis.
    Zone: 2 parallel planes / cylinder.
  • Perpendicularity —— 垂直度
    Symbol: ⊥
    Meaning: 90° to datum plane/axis.
    Zone: 2 parallel planes / cylinder.
  • Angularity —— 倾斜度
    Symbol: ∠
    Meaning: At specified basic angle to datum.
    Zone: 2 parallel planes at basic angle.
    4) LOCATION（Require Datum）
  • Position —— 位置度（最常用）
    Symbol: ⊕
    Meaning: Center/axis location relative to datums.
    Zone: Cylinder (usually ⌀tolerance).
  • Concentricity —— 同轴度
    Symbol: ◎
    Meaning: Median points coaxial with datum axis.
    Zone: Cylinder about datum axis.
  • Symmetry —— 对称度
    Symbol: ≡
    Meaning: Median plane symmetric to datum.
    Zone: Two parallel planes symmetric about datum.
    5) RUNOUT（Require Datum Axis）
  • Circular Runout —— 圆跳动
    Symbol: ↗
    Meaning: Variation in one revolution at one cross-section.
    Zone: Radial displacement at one section.
  • Total Runout —— 全跳动
    Symbol: ⌰
    Meaning: Variation along entire surface in rotation.
    Zone: Two concentric cylinders.

IMPORTANT MODIFIERS（常用修饰符）

  • MMC —— Maximum Material Condition
    Symbol: Ⓜ
    Meaning: Largest size for external features, smallest for internal.
  • LMC —— Least Material Condition
    Symbol: Ⓛ
    Meaning: Smallest size for external features, largest for internal.
  • RFS —— Regardless of Feature Size（默认，不写）
    Meaning: Tolerance same at any size.
  • Projected Tolerance Zone —— 延伸公差带
    Symbol: Ⓟ
    Meaning: Zone extends away from feature (for pins/studs).
  • Diameter Symbol —— 直径符号
    Symbol: ⌀
    Meaning: Tolerance zone is cylindrical.

FEATURE CONTROL FRAME (FCF) FORMAT

Example:⊕ ⌀0.1 Ⓜ A B C
1: Symbol (Position)
2: Tolerance value (diameter 0.1)
3: Modifier (MMC)
4–6: Datum references (A, B, C)

FUNDAMENTAL GD&T RULES（ASME Y14.5）

  1. All dimensions must have a tolerance (except reference/min/max/stock).
  2. Dimensions & tolerances fully define each feature; no assumed measurements.
  3. Basic dimensions have no tolerance; they define true position/angle.
  4. Datums are perfect; they are the origin for measurements.
  5. Form controls do not use datums; orientation/location/runout require datums.
  6. Position tolerance is usually cylindrical (use ⌀).
  7. Profile can control form, orientation, and location in one callout.
  8. MMC/LMC only apply to features of size (holes, pins, slots).

QUICK USE GUIDE

  1. Flatness: Gasket/sealing surfaces.
  2. Straightness: Shafts, edges.
  3. Circularity: Bearing races, rings.
  4. Cylindricity: Pistons, hydraulic bores.
  5. Profile: Complex 3D shapes, molds.
  6. Parallelism: Guide rails, mounting surfaces.
  7. Perpendicularity: Holes normal to a plate.
  8. Angularity: 45° brackets, bevels.
  9. Position: Hole patterns, bolt circles (#1).
  10. Concentricity: Rotating shafts, pulleys.
  11. Symmetry: Slots, tabs, symmetrical parts.
  12. Runout: Wheels, gears, rotating components.
